# FAQ — International Access and Verification

## Why do some students struggle to get the discount?

Student verification flows depend on **identity providers**, **school email formats**, and **regional availability** of partner checks. A student studying abroad, on a gap year, or with a nonstandard `.edu` pattern may hit edge cases that require manual review.

## What should ambassadors do first?

Confirm they are using the **official** student offer link and that their **name and institution** match what the verifier expects. Typos in legal name vs transcript name are a common failure mode.

## What if verification keeps failing?

Escalate through your **Campus Lead** or internal support channel with: country, institution, error code or screenshot (redact personal IDs), and whether they are enrolled full-time. Do not promise timelines you cannot control—set expectation of **manual review**.

## Events and inclusivity

When promoting workshops, add: “If verification blocks you, still come—we’ll help you use the editor and point you to support.” Access friction should not exclude students from **learning the tool**.

## Privacy reminder

Never collect passports or national IDs in ad-hoc spreadsheets. Route sensitive documents only through approved support paths.
